Description

This creamy and delicious chicken alfredo is a classic Italian dish. It combines tender chicken, rich alfredo sauce, and pasta for a satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast halves
  • Olive Oil
  • Italian style dressing
  • Clove garlic
  • Cream cheese
  • Condensed cream of chicken soup
  • Chopped mushrooms
  • Spaghetti
  • Fresh Parsley

Directions

  1. Cook chicken with water and dressing
  2. Whisk cheese and soup together in bowl
  3. Pour over chicken and cook together
  4. Boil spaghetti in salted water
  5. Combine and add parsley
  6. Serve
