Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that is a family favorite. It is a layered pasta dish with alternating layers of pasta, cheese, and a variety of other ingredients such as meats and vegetables. This recipe is a traditional lasagna recipe, but there are many variations that you can explore.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Diced onion
  • Tin canned tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Directions

  1. Brown the meat and onion
  2. Add the tomatoes, herbs, sugar, and spices
  3. Simmer the sauce
  4. Cook the noodles
  5. Mix the cheese and eggs
  6. Layer the lasagna
  7. Bake the lasagna
